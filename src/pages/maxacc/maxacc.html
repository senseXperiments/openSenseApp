The Quarterback Challenge

Who's got what it takes to be a future NFL Quarterback?

Put your senseBox in the senseBox soft football and compete against fellow players! Hit and you will get about three seconds to throw the ball and accelerate the senseBox MCU as fast as possible. Then it's your opponent's turn. Hand him the football with senseBox MCU and hit and you will see if your opponent can beat your acceleration record!
