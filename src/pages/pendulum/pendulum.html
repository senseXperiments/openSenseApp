Pendulum Experiment for measuring acceleration

The pendulum

This is an example for a setup of a pendulum.

The pendulum can be used to measure the acceleration.

What do you need?

SenseBox MCU Spirit level Cable ties Powerbank Nail or pen or screwdriver Use the cable ties to attach the senseBox MCU and the powerbank to the spirit level. Connect the powerbank to the senseBox MCU.

The following picture can give you an impression of the setup.

How does it work?

We used a nail which was placed in a solid wall. It is also possible to use a pen or a screwdriver to hold the pendulum. We hang the spirit level on the nail, in our case, the spirit level had a hole. We then raised the spirit level with the attached senseBox and powerbank to one side and let it swing.
