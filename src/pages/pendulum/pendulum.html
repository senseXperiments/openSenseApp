Experiment

Objective

To determine the pendulum´s acceleration from Accelerometer

Apparatus:

Sense box tool with MCU (Accelerometer sensor) as weight and string,pendulum hanger, stopwatch, and meter stick(ruler), scissor ,Adhesive tape

Procedure

  • Attach MCU at one ende of the string
  • Attach the other end of string to a suitable hanger for the Pendulum to swing freely
  • Let the Pendulum swing freelly to and from the direction for few menutes
  • Allow the possible movement of the MCU in different directions (X,Y,Z)
