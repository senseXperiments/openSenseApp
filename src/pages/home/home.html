Welcome to our SenseXperiments App

This App has been developed in a study project during the winter semester 2018/2019 at the Institute for Geoinformatics in MÃ¼nster.

The SenseXperiments App makes use of the BMX055 sensor of the sensebox MCU. The sensor provides a 3-axis accelerometer, a 3-axis-gyroscope and a magnetometer

In the menu we offer the following content:

When you click on AccelerationYou can measure the acceleration and visualize the measurements

By clicking on Game you can start the competition with a friend to find out who reaches the higher value of acceleration

The Gyroscope visualizes the measured gyroscope data, but that part of our App is still under construction
